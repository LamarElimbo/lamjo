Scory Story 1.0 is the near paramount of text classification software. With the potential of becoming StereoMood on steroids, Scory Story 1.0 offers the ability to not just select music based on your mood but to project yourself into the life of an on-screen character with a soundtracked life.


Simply enter in a sentence based on how you feel, what you're doing or what's going on around you and Scory Story will decide what type of movie scene that would be and offer up a genre and song choice to match.
