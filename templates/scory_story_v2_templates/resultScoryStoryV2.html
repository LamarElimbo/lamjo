On this page you can scroll through the phone to read your story and a soundtrack corresponding with the paragraghs will play in the background.


Understanding this Page

The dotted red line behind the phone indicates where the program assumes you are at in the story.

The first column of literary scenes is what Scory Story classified your current paragraph as.

The second column indicates the literary scene that Scory Story will retrive music from. This column accomodates for subtle changes in tone that may occur. For example, if paragraph 1 is a flirtatious scene and paragraph 2 is a romantic scene, the same song should play since they are similar enough in tone not to require a song change. This allows for fewer song changes and smoother transitions throughout the story.
