Scory Story 2.0 is a program that automatically creates soundtracks for written works by analyzing a piece of text and implementing a multinomial Naive Bayes based algorithm to classify paragraphs into literary scenes (e.g., romance scene, fight scene, etc...).

An algorithm is used to blend paragraphs with similar genres together so that song change occurs at an appropriately decreased rate.



Instructions


1: Either click on a fairy tale or paste your own story, separated by paragraphs, in the iphone.

2: Click Submit

3: Wait for a few seconds to be redirected to a page where your story will be freshly accessorized with music scores (duration of analysis is correlated to the size of the story).
